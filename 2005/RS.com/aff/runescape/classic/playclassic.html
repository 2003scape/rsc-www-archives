What is RuneScape Classic?
RS-Classic is a previous version of RuneScape which we've decided to keep online so you can see what RuneScape used to look like. As we're continually developing and updating RuneScape it's changed quite a lot over the years so RS-Classic is kept here for nostalgia reasons, and for those who prefer a simpler unchanging experience.

Whilst RS-Classic may look very similar to RuneScape in many areas and you use the same account to play, the games are not linked in any way. Bear in mind that any items or stats that you gain or lose in RS-Classic will not affect your real RuneScape character in any way, and will not count towards your ranking on the main hiscores.

Also please note that RS-Classic is no longer being updated, since obviously if we added all our updates then it would just become identical to the main game. If you wish to play with regular updates then we recommend you play the main RuneScape game which has already had many updates added, with more in progress. (e.g. new combat-system, new graphics, new quests, new skills, etc..)
