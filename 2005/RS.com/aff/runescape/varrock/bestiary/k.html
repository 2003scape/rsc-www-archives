Weight: How long is a piece of string? How heavy is a rock? What about a rock hard Dragon? We'll let you ask him

  Knight Of Ardougne

Height: 6ft 1in
Weight: 220 lbs
Varieties: Level 46
Locations: City Of Ardougne
Appearance: Human... underneath all the armour.
Way of Life: Becoming a Knight has long been a favoured career choice for Ardougne's strongest and bravest young men, with their shiny armour and adherence to the rules of chivalry gaining them a certain reputation with the ladies.
Likes: Testing their prowess against each other in jousting duels for ladies handkerchiefs.
Dislikes: People who ignore their carefully constructed rules of chivalry
