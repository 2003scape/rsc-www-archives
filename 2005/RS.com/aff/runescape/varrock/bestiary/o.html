  Ogre

Height: 7 ft
Weight: 300 lbs
Varieties: Level 48 Level 53 Level 55 Level 60 Level 70 Level 74
Locations: Small numbers in Kandarin, although they have mostly been wiped out or captured by humans. Large numbers are located in the lands known as the Feldip Hills or "Ogre Country" to the south of Kandarin
Appearance: Tall and muscular
Way of Life: Brown skinned ogres form tribes and are primitive in nature. They tend to lumber around in a world of their own. It may be that they are deep in thought. But this seems unlikely
Likes: Attacking people smaller than them.
Dislikes: Being wiped out by humans

  Otherworldly Being

Height: N/A
Weight: N/A
Varieties: Infinite
Locations: The otherworldly beings can be found in the Lost City of Zanaris. Wherever that is.
Appearance: Otherworldly. Although some say they look like a cross between a ghost and a witch

Likes: Again, we don't really know this yet, but on face value these beings seem pretty happy just to be alive. Is that something that they could teach us?
Dislikes: Being attacked by other beings who come from another world other than the one inhabited by the otherworldly being: e.g. people like you. Whatever the outcome, such mindless displays of violence can't be doing much for intergalactic relations
