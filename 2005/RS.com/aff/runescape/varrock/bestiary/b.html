  Bandit

Height: 5 ft 11 in
Weight: 235 lbs
Varieties: Level 21
Locations: The Wilderness
Appearance: Human
Way of Life: They live by the unique Bandits' Code of stealing from the rich and giving to the poor. If you define 'the poor' as them and 'the rich' as everyone else.
Likes: Shiny gold coins. And lots of them. Kept in easy to open moneybags.
Dislikes: The long arm of the Law.

  Barbarian

Height: 6 ft 2 in
Weight: 235 lbs
Varieties: Level 7
Level 8
Locations: The Barbarian Village
Appearance: Muscle-clad bearded warriors
Way of Life: Self-sufficient. Having plundered and pillaged in the tribal warfare of days gone by, some contemporary barbarians have now dropped anchor with settlements of their own. Remaining relatively neutral, they could be a useful force indeed if anyone could tempt them into an alliance...
Likes: Wood-chopping
Dislikes: Anyone who uses magic in battle

  Bear

Height: 5 ft (8ft at full stretch)
Weight: 600 lbs
Varieties: Level 19
Level 21
Locations: Bears like to roam the forests
Appearance: Large and deceptively cuddly looking
Way of Life: Bears are happiest when minding their own business, but they live in a world which values their fur. If disturbed by an adventurer in search of loot, these noble beasts pack quite a punch!
Likes: Hibernating
Dislikes: Fur-lined jackets

  Black Dragon

Height: 32 ft long
Weight: See Red Dragon
Varieties: Level 227
Locations: Taverley Dungeon
These awesome destroyers can usually be identified by the large pile of human bones that tend to surround them
Appearance: Jet black. That reddy-orange tint is actually just an optical illusion, because one is normally viewing them through a torrent of flames
Way of Life: King-like
Likes: Their long-held supremacy, which is now waning
Dislikes: The non-stop nature of evolution itself ...as shown by the recent emergence of the King Black Dragon

  Black Knight

Height: 6 ft on the nose
Weight: 200 lbs exactly: robust but fighting fit
Varieties: Level 33
Locations: Black Knight Fortress in the north, Taverley Dungeon, and the Lava Maze (wilderness)
Appearance: Large and foreboding, it is no wonder that these bad boys were once the talk of Runescape. And even now they continue to swing their weapons with the full weight of their illustrious history.
Way of Life: Educated and refined, these dashing villains like to unwind over a glass of mulled wine and Baxtorian poetry. They spit on peasants and refuse to pay taxes - but they still know how to treat a lady
Likes: Mulled wine and poetry. Remember this if ever you lack the motivation to decapitate someone
Dislikes: Peasants and taxes

  Blue Dragon

Height: 5 ft
Weight: 290 lbs
Varieties: Level 48
Level 111
Locations: Taverley Dungeon
Appearance: As their name suggests, these magnificent creatures can be identified by their distinctive sky blue appearance
Way of Life: The Blue Dragons guard the early stages of Taverley Dungeon. A beautiful yet fearsome creature, these guardians are but a taste of things to come
Likes: The tranquility of Taverley Lake. But prized for their flesh, these beasts now take refuge in the dungeon, where would-be attackers must fight the dragon on his own terms
Dislikes: Rangers
