Treasure Trails

It is said that the gods occasionally give players treasure, whether these are acts of random kindness or if they simply get bored is unclear. What is known is that when certain creatures die there is a chance that they will drop a clue.

This clue could lead you to someone in RuneScape, a location that you need to dig using a spade or something you need to search, such as a crate or chest.

Once you have solved the clue you will receive another clue, thus leading you on the trail closer to the reward: your treasure!

It is also known that the toughness of the creature that dropped the clue relates to how tough and long the trail will be.
