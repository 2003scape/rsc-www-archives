Agility Arena

The enterprising Cap'n Izzy No-Beard invites you to the Brimhaven Agility Arena, entry is only 200 gp! The entrance is the bamboo hut in the north-east part of Brimhaven. This is a members only feature.

The Agility Arena is a huge cavern of platforms connected by traps and obstacles. There are many different routes to get around the agility arena, you are much more likely to fail the obstacles on some routes than on others, some ways can not even be taken until you reach a certain agility level.

One platform is marked by a flashing arrow, try and make your way to it. Every minute the flashing arrow will move to a new pillar. If you tag more than one active pillar in a row then you will receive a ticket. You won't get a ticket at the first pillar but you will for every pillar you tag after that. If you miss a pillar then you will miss out on the next ticket so try and get as many as you can!

Whilst you are in the arena there is an indicator in the top right that shows you whether you tagged the last pillar. It starts red, when you tag the active pillar it will turn green, whilst it stays green you will receive a ticket when you tag the pillar. If you miss the pillar then it will turn back to red again and you will have to get it back to green before you can get another ticket.

You don't get much experience for getting past the obstacles, most of your experience comes from trading in the tickets you have collected. You can also trade in tickets for some items you can't get anywhere else including the herbs Toadflax and Snapdragon. (See Herblore).

The easy obstacles do not have an agility requirement, the medium obstacles require level 20 agility and the hard obstacles require level 40 agility. As your agility level increases you can take more routes through the arena and you will start to fail the traps and obstacles less often. You will begin to get significantly more tickets. Some words of warning: there are a couple of traps that require you to use your head; the hardest trap of all fires darts at you that will lower your agility if they hit you.

There be booty to be got, ye landlocked landlubbers!
