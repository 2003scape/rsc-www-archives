Woodcutting

Woodcutting is a skill used to cut logs from trees. These logs can be used in fletching and firemaking.

To chop down a tree, just left click on a tree to select chop. If you have an axe in your inventory and a sufficient woodcutting level you will attempt to chop down the tree.

More powerful axes will increase your chance of cutting down the tree.

A similar table to the one below can be opened in the game by clicking the woodcutting skill icon in the stats menu.

Woodcutting level requirements.

Ordinary treelevel 1
Oak treelevel 15
Willow treelevel 30
Maple tree (Members only)level 45
Hollow tree (Members only)level 45
Yew treelevel 60
Magic tree (Members only)level 75
