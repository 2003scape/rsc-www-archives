How to Cancel your Runescape Subscription


Credit Card Subscribers
If you have subscribed to RuneScape using a credit or debit card subscription through Worldpay, then there are two easy ways to cancel your subscription.

Option 1 The quickest and easiest way is to Log-in to your account and to cancel your subscription from there.
Option 2 If you can't remember your username/password you can still cancel by providing your card number. Click here

Direct Debit Subscribers
For customers using a direct debit subscription through PayByCash, you will need to cancel your subscription through the PayByCash website.
