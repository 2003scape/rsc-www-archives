  Chaos Druid

Height: 5 ft 8 in
Weight: 210 lbs
Varieties: Level 13
Locations: Taverly Dungeon
Appearance: They dress in the distinctive blood red robes of Zamorak
Way of Life: Masters of herblore, their friendly demeanour belies a dangerous knowledge of the dark arts
Likes: Using runestones as a source of darkness
Dislikes: Religious factions. As Chaos Druids, they have links with both Guthix and Zamorak. Being something of an unknown quantity, the chaos druids could be the ones to tip the balance in a major struggle

  Chaos Druid Warrior

Locations: Yanille Dungeon

  Chaos Dwarf

Height: 4 ft 3 in.
Weight: 220 lbs
Varieties: Level 48
Locations: Chaos dwarves can be found in dungeons and the wilderness
Appearance: Slightly heftier than their good ancestors, these dwarves clearly have an appetite for destruction. But more remarkable is their reddish hue, a clear sign of their beserker tendencies
Way of Life: Homicidal. It is believed that the chaos dwarves have been touched by Zamorak himself
Likes: Slamming an axe in your face
Dislikes: Polite conversation

  Chicken
