  Hero

Height: 6ft 2in
Weight: 205 lbs
Varieties: Level 69
Locations: Hero's can be found in the cosmopolitan city of Ardougne, secretly hoping for some injustice to be perpetrated so that they can pounce to the rescue
Appearance: With perfect abs and a well-chiselled jaw line, these All-Runescapian Heroes make you want to up your levels. If only so that you can do something to wipe that smug smile off their face

Likes: A challenge

  Highwayman

Height: 6 ft
Weight: 200 lbs
Varieties: Level 5
Locations: At major junctions: south of Draynor manor, and between Rimmington and Falador
Appearance: Stylish, cape-wearing human
Way of Life: Legendary. The highwayman is elegant and dashing: the thinking man's mugger. Although that isn't usually much consolation when he nicks your valuables
Likes: To prey on unsuspecting travellers, especially tourists
Dislikes: The idea that he should travel on a horse... This can only be a myth
